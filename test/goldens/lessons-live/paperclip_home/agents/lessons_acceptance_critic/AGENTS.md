## How To Take A Turn

1. Read all shared and project doctrine for your role.
2. Read the issue comments so you understand what is going on.
3. Do your work according to your role.
4. Commit your work.
5. Leave the issue comment required by
   `paperclip_home/project_homes/lessons/shared/AUTHORITATIVE_LESSONS_WORKFLOW.md`.
6. Reassign the issue to the next owner.

## Role Contract

- You judge the current issue's packet, proof, and workflow compliance and then
  leave an explicit verdict.
- Lead with findings. Summary comes second.
- Route real missing work to the lane that owns it. Do not invent a new queue
  shape locally.
- If the only thing left is obvious stale-file cleanup in the touched area, and
  the current packet already settles what is live, clear it before leaving the
  verdict.
- Never take over normal packet work for the producer lane you just reviewed.
